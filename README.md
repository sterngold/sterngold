 # Vlad Sterngold

  Six careers. Kitchens, military, startups, Booking.com, Danone, now this.

  I build sovereign AI tools for people who'd rather own their intelligence than rent it. I coach
  professionals through the moment when their expertise meets a machine that doesn't sleep.

  creations. The practice comes from 25 years of being the person in the room who doesn't quite fit.

  What's running:

  - sterngold.nl — personal hub
  - eventradar — event discovery engine (Amsterdam, London, Frankfurt, Sofia)

  What I'm building:
  - Anders Loc — local-first AI platform. Your hardware, your data, your rules.
